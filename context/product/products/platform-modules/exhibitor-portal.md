## 1. One-liner

A self-serve exhibitor and sponsor portal that lets organizers categorize sponsors by tier, lets exhibitors build branded booths with logos, videos, documents, and job postings through guided wizards, and gives both sides a unified workflow for booth setup, lead capture, networking, analytics, and post-event follow-up across virtual, in-person, and hybrid events.

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: Can sponsors and exhibitors track their performance during the event?**
Yes, vFairs offers robust analytics and reporting tools that allow sponsors and exhibitors to track booth traffic, attendee interactions, and content engagement. This data helps them measure ROI and make informed decisions during and after the event.

**Q: How does vFairs enhance sponsor and exhibitor visibility?**
vFairs provides multiple features to boost sponsor and exhibitor visibility, including branded booths, banner placements, and featured listings. Additionally, we offer opportunities for sponsors to gain prime exposure through customized sessions, push notifications, and promotional content across the platform.

**Q: What networking opportunities does vFairs offer for sponsors and exhibitors?**
vFairs enables rich networking opportunities through features like live chat, video meetings, and matchmaking tools. Exhibitors can connect with attendees in real-time, schedule appointments, and participate in virtual networking lounges, ensuring meaningful interactions throughout the event.

**Q: How does vFairs support sponsors and exhibitors in hybrid events?**
vFairs seamlessly integrates both virtual and onsite elements, allowing sponsors and exhibitors to manage their presence across hybrid events. They can engage with in-person and virtual attendees through a unified platform, ensuring consistent branding & messaging while maximizing reach and impact.
