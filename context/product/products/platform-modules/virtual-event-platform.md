## 1. One-liner

An end-to-end virtual event platform that runs online conferences, trade shows, and job fairs with a choice of immersive 3D or streamlined 2D environments, scalable live or on-demand sessions, AI-powered networking, virtual booths, real-time engagement tools, and accessibility built in, supporting up to 100,000+ concurrent attendees.

## 3. Capability Map

### 3.1 Virtual Environments
- **Immersive 3D environments** with entrance, lobby, exhibit hall, auditorium, networking lounges
- **Streamlined 2D layouts** for fast, no-fuss programs
- **Custom-branded lobbies, exhibit halls, auditoriums, networking lounges**
- **Customizable avatars and dress-code themes**
- **Sponsor banner, ad, and logo placement** throughout the venue
- **Floor plan builder** with seat plans and booth links

### 3.2 Webinars and Sessions
- **Live, semi-live, or on-demand webinars** at scale
- **Multi-track agenda management**
- **Personalized session recommendations**
- **Searchable on-demand library** post-event
- **Dedicated speaker portal** for bio and handout uploads
- **Polls, Q&A, and live reactions** inside sessions
- **Session ratings and surveys**

### 3.3 Virtual Booths and Exhibit Halls
- **Customizable booth tiers and sponsorship packages**
- **Product catalogs** with filter, search, and checkout
- **Live booth-rep chat, audio, and video**
- **Queued chat system** to prevent missed conversations
- **Video, brochures, product catalogs, job postings** supported per booth
- **Per-exhibitor lead and engagement reports**
- **Dedicated exhibitor portal**

### 3.4 Networking and Matchmaking
- **AI-powered matchmaking** based on profile, interests, and behavior
- **1:1 and group audio / video meeting tools**
- **Attendee-to-attendee and attendee-to-exhibitor networking**
- **Networking lounges**
- **Topic-based roundtables**
- **Speed networking capabilities**
- **Chat (queued and direct)**

### 3.5 Engagement and Gamification
- **Live polls, Q&A, real-time reactions** in sessions
- **Leaderboards**
- **Scavenger hunts**
- **Trivia**
- **Spin-the-wheel**

### 3.6 Registration and Ticketing

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: How many attendees can vFairs support at one event?**
vFairs scales from intimate events to global conferences with 100,000+ concurrent attendees. Our infrastructure has powered 50,000+ events for over 100 million attendees worldwide.

**Q: Can I host both 3D and 2D virtual events on vFairs?**
Yes. Choose immersive 3D environments with entrance, lobby, exhibit hall, auditorium for flagship events, or streamlined 2D layouts for fast, no-fuss programs. Both come fully branded, with the same engagement tools and the same support team.

**Q: What engagement and networking features come built in?**
Live polls, Q&A, and reactions in every session. Gamification through leaderboards, scavenger hunts, trivia, and spin-the-wheel. AI-powered matchmaking, 1:1 audio/video meetings, group chat, networking lounges, and topic-based roundtables. Engagement isn't an add-on at vFairs, it's the architecture.
