## 1. One-liner

A mobile lead capture app that lets exhibitors and sponsors scan attendee QR badges or business cards, qualify and score leads with custom forms and voice notes, sync data into HubSpot, Salesforce, Marketo, Zoho, Mailchimp, or Zapier in real time, and prove event ROI through per-exhibitor dashboards.

## 3. Capability Map

### 3.1 Lead Capture Methods
- **QR code scanning** from attendee badges
- **Business card capture** with auto-population
- **Manual data entry** fallback
- **Real-time data sync** with CRM systems
- **Lead enrichment via third-party data sources** — when an exhibitor scans a badge, vFairs pulls additional firmographic and contact fields (e.g. industry, company URL, company size) from a third-party enrichment provider based on what's available at event time, adding depth to each lead and enabling better profiling and prioritization without manual research

### 3.2 Lead Management
- **Custom lead capture forms** per exhibitor or per booth
- **Search and filter tools** to find leads fast
- **Lead scoring** based on preset criteria
- **Custom tagging system** (Hot, Warm, Cold or custom)
- **Written and voice notes** against each lead
- **Lead assignment** to specific sales team members
- **Multiple lead type support**
- **Conditional logic** in lead capture forms
- **Multi-select fields** in lead forms

### 3.3 Exhibitor Setup and Permissions
- **Exhibitor portal** with full booth and lead setup control
- **Sales team collaboration** with multi-user access per booth
- **Sales team invitation** workflow

- **Restrict user types from exporting leads**
- **Restrict export to custom email**
- **Limit export access to specific roles**
- **Allow exhibitors to manage their own lead forms**

### 3.4 Engagement Tracking
- **Chat log history** with each lead

### 3.5 Communications
- **Personalized email** to leads from within the app

### 3.6 Data and Analytics
- **Real-time dashboards** per exhibitor

- **Bulk and individual lead exports**

- **Real-time sync** with major CRMs

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: What is the vFairs Event Lead Capture App?**
The vFairs Event Lead Capture App is a mobile solution designed to help exhibitors and organizers efficiently collect and manage attendee information during events. It offers features like QR code scanning, business card capture, manual data entry, and real-time syncing with CRM systems.

**Q: What is the overall lead capture process using this app?**
The lead capture process using the vFairs Event Lead Capture App involves scanning attendee badges or QR codes, manually entering data, or capturing business card details. Once the data is collected, leads can be categorized, scored, and tagged with notes for easy follow-up, while real-time syncing ensures seamless integration with CRM systems for post-event outreach.

**Q: What is the difference between "lead capture" and "lead retrieval"?**
"Lead capture" refers to the process of collecting attendee information during an event, typically using methods like QR code scanning, business card capture, or manual entry. On the other hand, "lead retrieval" is a more specific term used for systems or tools that allow exhibitors to access and retrieve the contact details of event attendees in real-time, often through scanning badges or QR codes.

**Q: What types of events is the Event Lead Capture App suitable for?**
The Event Lead Capture App is suitable for a wide range of events, including trade shows, conferences, exhibitions, job fairs, and networking events. It is particularly beneficial for events where exhibitors or sponsors need to efficiently gather and manage leads, track engagement, and follow up with potential clients or partners.

**Q: How does the app simplify onsite contact capture for exhibitors?**
The app simplifies onsite contact capture for exhibitors by enabling them to quickly scan attendee QR codes or badges, automatically populating lead information. This eliminates the need for manual data entry, saves time, and ensures accurate and real-time data collection that can be immediately used for follow-up.

**Q: Can exhibitors add notes or categorize leads? Is there a lead grading mechanism in the app?**
Yes, exhibitors can add notes and categorize leads within the app, allowing them to personalize their follow-up strategy. The app also features a lead grading mechanism, enabling exhibitors to score leads based on their level of interest or potential, making it easier to prioritize follow-ups after the event.

**Q: How do organizers set up and configure the app for an event?**
Organizers can set up and configure the app by customizing event-specific information, such as attendee badges, QR codes, and lead capture forms. The app allows them to define data fields, set up lead scoring, and integrate with CRM systems to ensure seamless data transfer and tracking throughout the event.

**Q: How do you track leads at an event using the vFairs platform?**
Exhibitors can track leads at an event using the vFairs Lead Capture App by scanning attendee QR codes or badges, which instantly populates attendee information. They can then categorize leads by assigning tags such as "Hot," "Warm," or "Cold," and add notes or voice memos to provide context for follow-up actions. The app also offers real-time analytics, allowing exhibitors to monitor scan counts and lead capture metrics, which helps in assessing engagement levels and ROI during the event.

**Q: What integrations does the Event Lead Capture App offer?**
The vFairs Event Lead Capture App offers seamless integrations with several leading CRM and marketing automation platforms, including Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Zoho CRM, Zapier, and Mailchimp.

**Q: Are the lead capture forms customizable? Can I change them for different exhibitors or booths?**
Yes. You can customize lead capture forms to include custom fields, questions, etc., and tailor them to different exhibitors or booths if needed.

**Q: How can I see how many leads I'm generating / event impact in real time?**
There are dashboards and reporting tools that show lead quality vs quantity, tracking by individual exhibitors or team members, follow-up status, etc. These are real-time during the event.

**Q: How do I get lead data out of the system? Is there CRM integration?**
Yup. The app supports real-time sync with major CRMs and marketing tools. You can also export data (bulk or individual leads) with filters (booth name, captured by, lead type, etc.).
