## 1. One-liner

A built-in event marketing engine for sending branded email campaigns, building custom landing pages, scheduling SMS and WhatsApp drips, segmenting audiences, and using an AI assistant to draft emails, social posts, landing pages, and press releases, all without a separate marketing automation tool or designer.

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: What do you mean by event marketing?**
Event Marketing deals with advertising an event beforehand to increase registrations and attract more users. It is an essential component for a successful virtual, hybrid, or in-person event.

**Q: What is the aim of event marketing?**
The main goal of event marketing is to drive new customers, increase registrations, engage with target audiences, and increase brand awareness.

**Q: Does vFairs have an event promotion platform?**
vFairs is an event management platform that offers event marketing and promotion via the vFairs Discover page. Any event hosted by vFairs gets featured on the Discover page and reaches a wider audience.
