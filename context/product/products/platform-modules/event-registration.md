## 1. One-liner

An end-to-end registration platform that lets organizers build branded event websites with drag-and-drop, capture payments through 30+ gateways, segment audiences for personalized communication, and feed real-time attendee data into the rest of the vFairs platform (mobile app, check-in, analytics).

## 3. Capability Map

### 3.1 Custom Form Building
- **Drag-and-drop form builder** with a wide variety of field types
- **Conditional (and-or) logic workflows** to surface or hide fields based on responses
- **Custom registration categories** so different audiences see personalized forms
- **Pre-defined form fields** for airline tickets, meal options, and accommodation
- **Attendee photo capture** for downstream facial recognition check-in
- **Domain-based registration controls** to prevent unauthorized sign-ups
- **Form field limits** and dropdown management
- **Multi-language registration forms**
- **WCAG-compliant** registration websites

### 3.2 Group Registration
- **Group sign-up flow** with full control of group data collection
- **Different ticketing tiers** for group packages or bulk orders
- **Group payment options** including assigning packages and add-ons to individual group members
- **Group reporting** with dedicated CSV exports

### 3.3 Branded Event Website
- **Multi-page event website** built with a drag-and-drop website builder
- **Customizable colors and fonts**
- **Template library** plus build-from-scratch option
- **Custom branded URL** hosting
- **Per-page go-live scheduling**
- **Multi-lingual websites**
- **Organization-level asset library** to reuse design across events
- **LinkedIn Community Builder integration**
- **Referral UTM codes** for lead attribution to specific campaigns

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: What is an event registration software?**
An online event registration software allows event hosts to collect important registrant information prior to the event. Registration software can also help hosts set up ticketing, collect payments, and kick off the pre-event user management process.

**Q: How do I create a registration form for an event?**
With vFairs, you can simply drag and drop a variety of form fields to your registration form and publish it on your event page. vFairs also offers a variety of value adds, such as payment integrations options for group registrations and automated user segmentation.

**Q: How do I create an event registration page?**
You can host your event registration form on a custom-branded event landing page with the help of a platform like vFairs. Once your audience lands on the dedicated event page, they will be able to access your event registration form with the click of a button.

**Q: Will I be able to create different ticket types and pricing options for my event?**
Yes, vFairs' registration process allows you to create multiple ticket types with varying prices and packages. You can set different pricing options for early bird registrations, VIP passes, or group discounts.

**Q: Can I integrate vFairs' registration process with other event management tools?**
Yes, vFairs' registration process can be easily integrated with other event management tools like event planning software, CRM systems, and marketing automation tools.

**Q: Can I do group registrations or allow attendees to pay in groups?**
Yes. Group registration is supported, plus the ability to set up different ticketing tiers or pricing for group packages or bulk orders.

**Q: How is customer support provided for setup and registration issues?**
vFairs offers dedicated project support to help build custom workflows, assist branding & setup, and ensure the registration and ticketing process runs smoothly.

**Q: Can I build a branded event registration page/website without coding?**
Yep. You get drag-and-drop page builders, themed templates, custom branding (colors, fonts, logos), your own URL, and tools to configure how pages show up and when.

**Q: Which payment methods does vFairs support?**
The platform supports secure payment gateways (e.g. Stripe, PayPal) as well as 30+ options via the Payment Marketplace. It's PCI DSS and GDPR compliant.
