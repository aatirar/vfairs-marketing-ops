## 1. One-liner

A speaker lifecycle platform that handles call for speakers, blind reviewer assignment, accepted-speaker portals for bio and material uploads, automated task and email follow-ups, and event-day broadcast tools, all connected directly to event registration, agenda, and the mobile app so speaker data flows without manual re-entry.

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: What is speaker management software?**
Speaker management software helps event teams collect speaker applications, review and approve submissions, onboard accepted speakers through a self-serve portal, and assign them to sessions. It replaces the combination of spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected form tools that most conference teams use today.

**Q: Does vFairs handle the full speaker lifecycle?**
Yes. vFairs covers the entire flow - launching a call for speakers, collecting and reviewing submissions, speaker profile onboarding, session assignment, communication, and in-session support on event day. Everything runs inside one platform, connected to your registration, agenda, and mobile app.

**Q: Can speakers complete their own profiles and upload materials?**
Yes. Accepted speakers get a dedicated portal where they fill out their bio, upload a headshot, add session materials, and complete any custom fields you have set up. You control which fields must be complete before a profile is published.

**Q: Does vFairs support blind review for speaker submissions?**
Yes. Reviewers evaluate submissions without seeing the submitter's name or organization. You can also assign reviewers by category or track, set weighted scoring criteria, and balance workloads across your review panel.

**Q: How does speaker data connect to the rest of my event?**
Speaker profiles feed directly into your event agenda, event website, and mobile app. When a speaker updates their bio or session description, it syncs automatically. There is no manual export and re-import between tools.

**Q: Does this work for in-person, virtual, and hybrid conferences?**
Yes. vFairs manages speakers across all formats. For virtual and hybrid sessions, speakers access the broadcast studio directly through the platform. For in-person events, their schedules and session details are available through the mobile event app.
