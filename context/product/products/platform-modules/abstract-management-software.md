## 1. One-liner

A customizable call-for-papers platform that collects abstract submissions through branded forms, runs blind, double-blind, or peer review with weighted scoring and bulk reviewer assignment, then converts accepted abstracts directly into agenda sessions through field-mapping, eliminating manual session setup.

## 3. Capability Map

### 3.1 Abstract Submission Forms
- **Drag-and-drop form builder** for custom submission forms
- **Per-abstract-type custom forms** with unique questions / fields
- **Automatic author, co-author, and submitter profile forms**
- **Dynamic form fields** that adjust with tagged references
- **Group submissions** by category, track, and type
- **Customize the default labels** of the abstract management module
- **Customize the color scheme** of the module

### 3.2 Deadlines and Edits
- **Submitter draft-saving** with edits allowed
- **Cutoff date per submission type**
- **Editing / submission extensions** via access codes
- **Co-author count limits** per submission
- **Information visibility controls** to submitters
- **Restrict submitter login access** based on access code creation

### 3.3 Reviewer Assignment
- **Manual or automatic reviewer assignment**
- **Bulk assignment**
- **Category- or track-based assignment**
- **Even workload distribution or round-robin**
- **Multi-layer evaluation** with peer reviewers, panel judges, lead reviewers
- **Centralized reviewer dashboard** for progress tracking
- **Auto reviewer assignment** capability for abstract submissions
- **Restrict lead reviewers** from accepting / rejecting submissions
- **Disable evaluation access** for lead reviewers

### 3.4 Review Types and Scoring
- **Blind review** (hide submitter details from reviewers)
- **Double-blind review** (hide both submitter and author identity)
- **Peer reviewer evaluations**
- **Panel judge final decisions** based on majority votes
- **Weight-based scoring** for judges to override peer decisions
- **Evaluation criteria dropdown values** configurable per abstract
- **Total evaluation score view** per reviewer
- **Undo reviewer decisions** capability

### 3.5 Session Conversion
- **Field-mapping** to convert abstracts into agenda sessions / webinars
- **Speaker bios, titles, presentation types** included on conversion
- **Downloadable handouts and session details**
- **Customize session content** for agenda needs
- **Bulk session generation** from accepted submissions

### 3.6 Speaker Management
- **Speaker details collected** through submitter, author, or co-author profiles
- **First-login profile completion requirement**
- **Direct assignment of accepted abstracts** to speaker slots
- **Automatic speaker-to-session linking**
- **Speaker list export** with submission and session data

### 3.7 Communications
- **Module-specific email templates**
- **Abstract email templates** configurable per stage
- **Email campaigns to module-specific user segments**
- **Auto-reminders for draft submissions**

### 3.8 Reporting and Analytics
- **CSV data export**
- **Reviewer performance and participation tracking**
- **Detailed logs** of submission edits, assignments, decisions
- **Real-time evaluation reports**
- **Progress tracking and outcome analysis**

### 3.9 Admin Controls
- **Access and review submissions** directly from the Event Owner Portal
- **Admin edit** of abstracts
- **Submission notes per entry**
- **Download abstract submission details** as admin
