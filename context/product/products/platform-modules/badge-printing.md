## 4. Deployment Options

| Mode | Hardware | Setup | Use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Staffed desk** | Tablet or laptop + supported printer | vFairs technician on-site or remote setup | Faster line throughput with staff assistance |
| **Self-serve kiosk** | Touch screen / tablet stand / floor unit + printer | Drag-and-drop kiosk design | Lower-staff overhead, branded self-service |
| **Pre-print bulk** | Backend or on-site printer | Pre-event setup | Large events with mostly pre-registered attendees |
| **On-demand at door** | On-site printer | Same-day setup | Walk-in heavy events |

Pre-print and on-demand can run in parallel on the same event.

## 10. FAQ (verbatim from marketing page)

**Q: What are event badges made of?**
Event badges are typically made of durable materials such as PVC or plastic, ensuring they withstand the rigors of event attendance.

**Q: Can event badges be customized to match branding/sponsor needs?**
Yes. The platform allows you to design custom badges - choose layouts (single or double-sided), adjust colors, fonts, images (logos, sponsor logos, attendee photos), include QR codes or custom fields.

**Q: What types of badge printing options are there (bulk vs on-demand)?**
Both are supported. You can pre-print badges in bulk (for example for attendees who pre-register), or print badges on demand when attendees arrive. There are also options for group printings (multiple badges in one go).

**Q: Which badge and printer types are supported / what should we pick?**
vFairs supports a variety of badge and printer types, depending on what you need: PVC plastic badges, paper badges (thermal / direct thermal), sticky labels, adhesive badges, etc. They also support specific printers (e.g. Zebra, Epson, Brother printer). Guides are provided to help you choose based on durability, print quality, cost, speed.

**Q: How fast is the check-in and badge printing process?**
With QR scanning or kiosks, check-in takes just seconds per attendee, minimizing wait times and keeping entry lines moving quickly.

**Q: What kind of analytics are available for check-in and badge printing?**
The platform provides real-time reporting on attendee arrivals, badge issuance, and session check-ins, along with downloadable post-event reports for deeper analysis.

**Q: Does the system support reprinting or editing badges on-site?**
Yes. If a badge is lost, damaged, or needs updating, staff can quickly reprint or edit attendee details from the check-in dashboard.

**Q: Can attendees edit their badge details after registration?**
Organizers can allow attendees to update specific fields (such as job title or organization name), ensuring badges are accurate before printing.

**Q: Is there a limit to the number of badges I can print?**
No. The system is scalable and supports unlimited badge printing, whether your event is 100 attendees or 100,000.

**Q: How does vFairs ensure the security of badge printing and attendee data?**
vFairs follows strict security protocols to safeguard attendee information. QR codes and barcodes are encrypted, and consent can be collected for features like facial recognition.

**Q: How are lost or damaged badges handled?**
Staff can easily search attendee details and reprint a replacement badge within seconds, ensuring minimal disruption.

**Q: Can I collect data from badge scans?**
Definitely. Badges can include QR codes for lead retrieval, session tracking, or engagement metrics. This data is captured in real time and can be exported post-event.
